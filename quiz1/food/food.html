Food in Gresik

Sego Karak

Karak is an old food that is usually combined with grated coconut and fried tempeh

Otak-otak Bandeng

Otak-otak Bandeng is a food made from milkfish and in it there are spices mixed with fish meat, the taste is usually spicy.

Nasi Krawu

Nasi Krawu is a food made from shredded chicken, which has a distinctive taste.

Martabak Usus

Martabak Usus is the same as martabak in general but contains chicken intestine meat that is cut into small pieces

Pudak

Pudak is a food made from rice flour mixed with spices and made by steaming and wrapped in areca leaves.

Lontong Roomo

Roomo lontong is a typical roomo food in the Gresik area, like porridge but orange in color and usually spicy
