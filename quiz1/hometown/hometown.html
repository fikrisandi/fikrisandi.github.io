About Gresik

Gresik is the name of a city in the province of East Java. The city of Gresik is located between the city of Surabaya and the city of Lamongan. Gresik city itself is the only city in Indonesia that has many names. Some of its nicknames are Pudak City, Milkfish City, Industry City and guardian or santri City.

Gresik Kota Pudak

Gresik is called Pudak City because Pudak is a typical food of Gresik city. This food is made from rice flour, sugar and coconut milk wrapped in Ope (betel nut (Pelepah Pinang)), then steamed. The people of Gresik city themselves usually use Pudak as snacks to treat various kinds of celebrations. Starting from wedding celebrations, circumcision, celebration of a new house, to death, Pudak is also used as a treat, besides that, tourists also make pudak as one of the typical souvenirs of the city of Gresik. Pudak Gresik has a distinctive taste compared to other foods. Pudak taste also varies; there is white pudak because it is without coloring and only uses granulated sugar, there is red pudak also without coloring ingredients using only brown sugar, there is sago pudak made from sago, and there is also green pandan pudak with original coloring from pandan leaves so that it smells typical pandan .

Besides the distinctive taste, the pudak wrapped in Ope has a distinctive shape as well. No other type of food is the same as the Pudak form. This is because Ope itself has begun to obtain steps and its manufacture is not simple. Ope derived from areca nut cannot be directly used as a Pudak wrapper. Ope must first be processed through various stages; starting from the betel nut which is cleaned, then cut into squares according to size, then folded in half and sewn on the other two sides until the Ope becomes a ready-to-use wrap.

Gresik Kota Wali / Santri

Two of the nine Walisongo are in the city of Gresik, including Sunan Giri and Sunan Maulana Malik Ibrahim. No wonder many people refer to Gresik as the city of guardians or the city of santri because of the existence of a guardian and identical with the spread of Islam in depth. Sunan Giri's Pesareans are located at the top of Mount Giri, the only mountain in the city of Gresik. There is a statement that says that all buildings in the city of Gresik must not exceed the height of Mount Giri, where Sunan Giri's market is located. If there is a building that is higher than Sunan Giri's market place, the building will collapse or will not last long. I don't know if this is true or just a myth, but strangely, there is not a single skyscraper in Gresik, unlike other big cities in general.

In addition, the gate of Sunan Giri's market place has become the pride icon of the city of Gresik. It is proven that when we just entered the Gresik city area from Surabaya, we will be treated to a replica of the typical Sunan Giri gate as a sign that we have entered the entrance gate of the Gresik city area. And then we will also be treated to the giant slogans of the city of Gresik which are lined up along Veteran Street such as; "Gresik City of Faith" and "Gresik Decorated with Faith".

Gresik Kota Bandeng

Talking about the traditions of the city of Gresik, we will never finish in one face-to-face because there are so many traditions that continue to be cultivated among the people of the city of Gresik. For example, at the end of the month of Ramadan, the traditional milkfish auction and malem selawe3 are held. Milkfish auction, one of the annual traditions that has never been abandoned by the people of Gresik. Every year at the end of the fasting month, the month of Ramadan, to be precise on the 28th of Ramadan night, or commonly known as Malem songolikur4, there is always an auction of milkfish. The auction, which is usually held along the Kota market street, aims to cultivate milkfish which is a typical fish of the city of Gresik as well as a prestigious event for milkfish lovers as a mandatory food during Eid.

In the auction, there were a lot of farmers in the city of Gresik who deliberately took care of their milkfish until they were large and worthy of being included in the auction. It is a matter of pride for the fishpond that their milkfish is the winner as the largest milkfish out of all the milkfish that have been put up for auction. The bigger and longer the milkfish, the more expensive it will be. One milkfish that weighs more than 5 kg with a length of more than one meter can reach a price of Rp. 5,000,000, -. Selewe night is an annual tradition of the people around the Sunan Giri area. This Selawe night activity is a kind of large-scale bazaar event which is held along the road to Sunan Giri's tomb. Starting from the Kebomas intersection to the Sekarkurung T-junction, all roads were blocked from the afternoon of the 24th of Ramadan, until the dawn of the 25th of Ramadan to be used as a place to hold the Selawe Night event.

Gresik Kota Industri

Gresik has an industrial area called the Gresik Industrial Estate (KIG). There are dozens of factories in the area that produce various products and provide a variety of morning jobs for most of the people of Gresik and its surroundings. Apart from KIG, there are also many other large factories in Gresik. There are PT Semen Gresik, PT Petro Kimia Gresik, PT Barata Indonesia, PT Karunia Alam Segar, PT Maspion, PT Wiharta Karya Agung, etc. Therefore, it is not surprising that the city of Gresik gets the title of Industrial City.The growing industry in the city of Gresik is not only in the form of large factories, many home industries are also developing in the city of Gresik. The growing home industry in the city of Gresik produces various types of needs for the people of the city of Gresik itself, such as; convection bags, gloves, koko clothes, etc.
